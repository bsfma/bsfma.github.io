Become a Vendor

Bonner Springs Farmers' Market is a producer based market, meaning that vendors must directly produce the majority of items offered. If you grow, make, bake, or produce your own products, and would like to join the Market, complete the appropriate application form and e-mail it to BonnerFarmersMarket@gmail.com. The Market Board will review your application and will contact you soon.

Vendors are allowed to supplement up to 20% of their offerings with items purchased from reputable, known, and approved sources. However, supplemented items must be clearly identified in the application and in your booth on market day. All agricultural products, including supplemental items, must be produced within 200 miles of the Market.
