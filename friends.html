Support the Market by Becoming a Member

Friends of the Market are market shoppers and volunteers who are passionate about bringing quality, local products to our community. Becoming a Friend of the Market helps support market programs and provides valuable access to healthy food for the citizens of Wyandotte County.

You can become a Friend of the Market by joining the Bonner Springs Farmers' Market Association, the organization that oversees and administers the Bonner Springs Farmers' Market. Annual membership is $20 and includes a Market tote bag or license plate of your choice. Members also have a voting seat at our Annual Market meeting and are eligible to serve on the Market Board.

Your membership dollars support market events, public outreach, and food assistance programs.

For more information on becoming a member or volunteering with the Market, e-mail BonnerFarmersMarket@gmail.com

To join, download the form below. You can bring or mail the form to the Bonner Springs Library (201 N. Nettleton) or stop by the market information booth on Market Day.
