Join Friends of the Market

Friends of the Market are market shoppers, vendors, and volunteers who are passionate about bringing quality, local products to our community. Joining Friends of the Market helps support market programs and provides valuable access to healthy food for the citizens of Wyandotte County.

Annual membership is $20 and includes a Market tote bag or license plate of your choice. Members also have a voting seat at our Annual Market meeting and are eligible to searve on Market Board.

Your membership dollars support market events, public outreach, and food assistance programs.

For more information on volunteering with the Market or Friends of the Market, e-mail BonnerFarmersMarket@gmail.com

To become a Friend of the Market, download the form below. You can bring or mail the form to the Bonner Springs Library (201 N. Nettleton) or stop by the market information booth on Market Day.
